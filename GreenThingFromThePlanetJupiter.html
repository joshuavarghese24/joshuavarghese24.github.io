Overview

Green Thing from the Planet Jupiter is a two-player competitive cat-and-mouse chase game starring a housecat named Cannoli, trying to stop an alien that crash-landed in his home's backyard. 

The alien, also known as "Green Thing", has to collect parts throughout the house that could be used to repair the UFO, wielding a Stun Gun for self-defense. Cannoli can swipe at Green Thing using his claws, or use an ACME-branded "Cat-Alogue" to place a wide array of traps to try and stop Green Thing.
